Frequently Asked Questions

Select your preferred Room, Check-in Date, Check-out Date and number of rooms. You may select up to maximum 10 rooms. Make sure to select the number of persons you want on each room. Then click on the “Check Rates & Availability” button. Select your preferred room type and package. You may fill in the specific number of rooms per room type. Once you're done selecting the numbers of your room(s), you will be directed to the next step; entering of Guest Details and Mode of Payment. Your booking summary will also be on this page. After entering the information needed, please make sure to read the Terms and Conditions. Once done and agreed, kindly tick the tick box and click on “Book Now’ button. An electronic confirmation voucher will be sent to your email. In case you haven’t received any email on your inbox, kindly check your junk/spam folder.

Yes, you can. Just key in the Guest Name (First Name and Last Name/Surname) when booking a room. Passport number for foreign guests, and any valid ID for Filipino locals, is required to be presented upon check-in.

We advise you to print out your Electronic Receipt for a smooth check in at the reception area. Otherwise, just present a valid identification and confirm your booking details at the front desk.

You can pay for your room online using a credit card. If you do not have a credit card, you also have the option to settle charges via bank deposit at any Robinsons Bank or BDO branch. Walk-in guests may pay over-the-counter at the Front Desk through cash or credit card. E-Wallets are also accepted (WeChat, AliPay, GCash, Grab Pay) in select properties.
