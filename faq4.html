Frequently Asked Questions

No, there is no need to make a security deposit upon check-in. However, you are encouraged to return your keycard to the Front Desk for the use of the next guest.

The standard check-in time is 14:00 hours while the check-out time is 12:00 hours. You may request for early check-in of 10 AM or late check-out until 2 PM but is subject to room availability. Additional room charges may be imposed for check-in and check-out earlier or later than stated.

Room transfers or changes are free of charge, subject to availability. You may request an upgrade from a Standard to a Superior room. Please note that upgrades are subject to availability and may incur an additional charge based on the rate difference.

Should you be unable to return the keycard, or wish to keep it as a souvenir, there will be a PHP 100.00 charge.

To avail of senior citizen’s discount or persons with disability discount, the qualified guest shall be personally present during check-in and shall present their Senior Citizen’s or PWD ID. The senior citizen’s discount and the persons with disability discount shall only be available for payments made at the property.
